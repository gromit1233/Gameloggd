What is Gameloggd?

Gameloggd is a site for games, on it you can, log your collection of games, show-off your favourite games and see the current top ranking games.

GL

Who Created Gameloggd and why was it Made?

The creator of Gameloggd, Bryan Zhong has been playing video games since he was a child, falling in love with games such as Iron Fist and Lord of Battle. He created Gameloggd due to the absence of any PWA that allows users to log their collection of games
